Welcome Condo

An apartment is a self-contained housing unit that occupies part of a building. It typically consists of multiple rooms such as bedrooms, a living room, a kitchen, and a bathroom, all within a single dwelling space. Apartments are usually found in multi-story buildings called apartment complexes or blocks.
