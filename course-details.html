Instructor Description

"Today, we’re looking at a typical modern apartment designed for urban living. A standard apartment unit usually includes one or more bedrooms, a living room, a kitchen, and one or more bathrooms. Most apartments today also feature air conditioning, hot water systems, and balconies that offer outdoor space. Apartments may be furnished or unfurnished, and they’re often located in buildings with shared facilities such as elevators, parking, and 24-hour security. Tenants typically pay monthly rent, and sometimes a deposit is required. When describing an apartment, it’s important to highlight its location, amenities, and suitability — for example, whether it’s ideal for families, couples, or working professionals. Let’s also remember to mention proximity to schools, markets, public transport, and entertainment venues, as these add great value to the apartment."
